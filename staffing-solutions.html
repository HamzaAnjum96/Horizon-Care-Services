Products and Services

Our range of staffing solutions and products are designed to meet the diverse needs of the individuals we care for and the organisations we work in partnership. Our core service revolves around providing staffing solutions for a wide range of health and social care settings, including hospitals, nursing homes, residential homes, supported living accommodations and community centres. We also provide home care to individuals in their own homes. These comprehensive offerings include:

  • Staffing: We have a portfolio of Registered Nurses, Social Workers, Occupational Therapists, Physiotherapists, Health Care Assistants, Support Workers and Administrative staff to offer support on short notice.
  • Assessment and Care Planning: We conduct comprehensive assessments to create highly individualised care plans. This holistic approach encompasses rigorous risk assessment and evaluates physical, emotional, and social needs to determine the most suitable level of care.
  • Care Coordination: Our nurses, social workers, Occupational Therapists, and Physiotherapists, are adept at coordinating care with other healthcare providers, facilitating a seamless and comprehensive care delivery system.
  • Personal Care: Our skilled staff provides invaluable assistance with daily activities, such as bathing, grooming, dressing, toileting, and mobility support, across various care settings.
  • Companionship: We extend companionship services to provide vital social interaction and emotional support to clients and their families, particularly those experiencing feelings of isolation.
  • Medication Management: Our services include careful oversight to ensure clients adhere to their prescribed medication regimens, promoting their health and well-being.
  • Meal Preparation: Our staff actively participate in meal planning and preparation, taking into account specific dietary restrictions and preferences, ensuring that clients enjoy nutritious and satisfying meals.
  • Housekeeping: Our dedicated staff provides essential light housekeeping services, including cleaning, laundry, and maintaining a safe and organized living environment.
  • Shopping: We offer assistance with shopping and various other essential shopping related chores, enhancing the overall quality of life for our clients.
  • Respite Care: We provide essential temporary relief to family and carer, allowing them the opportunity to recharge and take well-deserved breaks.
  • Specialist support: Dementia and Alzheimer’s Care: Our specialised staff provide compassionate care for individuals with dementia or Alzheimer’s disease, focusing on their unique needs and challenges while safeguarding their comfort and well-being. Hospice Care: With compassion at the forefront, we offer dedicated care for individuals facing terminal illnesses, extending support to both patients and their families during these challenging times.
