Our Philosophy of Care

At Horizon Care Services, our dedicated team is driven by a strong commitment to maintaining the highest standards of care. Our philosophy revolves around a set of core values that guide everything we do:

  • Holistic and Person-Centred Approach: Our approach towards care is holistic, transparent, and person-centred. We understand that each person is unique, with their own set of needs and preferences. Our care and risk management plans are tailored to the individual, ensuring that they receive the right support at the right time, and we respond promptly to the ever-changing needs of the individual.
  • Respect and Dignity: We deeply respect our clients’ dignity and privacy. We recognise the importance of individual choices, cultural backgrounds, social preferences, and religious beliefs. We are dedicated to preserving the independence of our clients and maximising their potential by involving them in their care to the fullest extent possible.
  • Compliance and Excellence: We are unwavering in our commitment to adhere to national standards and regulations, as well as our own internal policies. Our employees undergo comprehensive training and are always ready to go the extra mile to provide the best possible care. Our goal is to enhance the quality of our clients’ lives through exceptional care.
  • Integration and Continuity: We recognise the importance of seamless care provision. Our diverse team actively participate in the care processes with individual and their support network. We coordinate and communicate with professionals, family and friends to ensure the continuity of care, promoting a consistent and reliable experience for people we care for.

By aligning ourselves with these principles, we ensure that every person we serve receives the highest quality of care, and we strive to make a positive impact on their lives. At Horizon Care Services, our commitment to excellence is at the heart of everything we do.
