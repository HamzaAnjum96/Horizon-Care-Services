About Us

A Compelling Vision for Care

Horizon Care Services is a dynamic health and social care provider with a compelling vision. Our mission is to deliver tailored, high-quality care that transforms lives while promoting respect, dignity, and individuality.

Our dedication to excellence is reflected in our highly trained staff, each of whom provides exceptional care customised to meet the distinct needs of every individual. By fostering collaboration with healthcare professionals, local communities, families, and carers, we ensure seamless integration and a continuous continuum of care for each person.

How We Work

Our Philosophy of Care

Our dedicated team is driven by a strong commitment to maintaining the highest standards of care.

Holistic & Person-Centred

Our approach is holistic, transparent, and person-centred. Care and risk management plans are tailored to the individual, ensuring right support at the right time.

Respect & Dignity

We deeply respect our clients' dignity and privacy, recognising individual choices, cultural backgrounds, social preferences and religious beliefs.

Compliance & Excellence

Unwavering commitment to national standards and regulations. Our employees undergo comprehensive training to provide the best possible care.

Integration & Continuity

Our diverse team actively coordinates with professionals, family and friends to ensure continuity of care and a consistent experience.
