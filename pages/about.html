About Horizon Care Services

We provide person-centred health and social care support rooted in dignity, consistency, and meaningful outcomes.

Our Mission

Horizon Care Services exists to provide tailored support that helps people feel safe, respected, and empowered in their everyday lives. We believe high-quality care starts with listening carefully, understanding each person as an individual, and building support around what matters most to them.

Our teams work across supported accommodation and staffing services, partnering with families, commissioners, and multidisciplinary professionals to deliver dependable care in real-world settings. We focus on continuity, clear communication, and thoughtful planning so that support remains consistent and responsive.

Above all, we are committed to care that is practical, compassionate, and accountable. From first referral through ongoing review, our approach is grounded in professionalism, safeguarding, and respect for each person’s rights, preferences, and goals.

What Guides Us

Our Values

These four principles shape every interaction, decision, and support plan we deliver.

Dignity & Respect

We protect privacy, uphold choice, and ensure each individual is treated with compassion and fairness at every stage of support.

Professionalism

We maintain clear standards in conduct, documentation, safeguarding, and communication so people can rely on us with confidence.

Collaboration

We work closely with service users, families, local authorities, and clinical teams to align support around shared goals and outcomes.

Continuity

We prioritise dependable staffing, regular reviews, and joined-up planning to reduce disruption and promote long-term progress.

Our Approach

Assessment-led, personalised, and regularly reviewed

We begin with holistic assessment, considering physical health, mental wellbeing, risk factors, and day-to-day support needs. This allows us to design practical care plans that are tailored rather than generic.

Each plan is reviewed in partnership with the individual and relevant professionals, so support can adapt when circumstances change. This structured but flexible approach helps maintain safety, confidence, and progress over time.

Regulatory Note

Horizon Care Services operates as a registered health and social care provider, working in compliance with CQC standards and UK GDPR.
